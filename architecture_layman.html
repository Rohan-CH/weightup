WeightUp: How It Works

A plain-English guide to the parts that make our fitness app run.

Author Rohan Chakkadath
Date Nov 2025
01 — The Building Blocks
Think of the app like a modern restaurant. Here is who does what:
🎨 The Front Desk & Dining Area
(React & Tailwind)
This is everything you see and touch on your screen. The buttons, the charts, and the colors. It makes sure the app looks beautiful and responds instantly when you tap something.
🏃 The Waiters & Security
(Next.js)
This is the middleman. It checks your ID at the door (makes sure you are logged in) before letting you into the private areas. It also carries your requests to the kitchen.
🗄️ The Secure Vault
(Supabase)
This is our massive, secure filing cabinet. It holds your passwords, profile pictures, and every single workout you've ever logged. It never forgets a number.
📈 The Artist
(Recharts)
A special tool that takes your boring numbers (like "100kg") and draws beautiful, easy-to-read progress lines so you can actually see yourself getting stronger.
02 — The Rooms in Our Gym
The app is divided into different "rooms". You must have a key (be logged in) to access most of them.
🚪 The Lobby (Login / Sign Up)
Where you prove who you are. The only room open to strangers.
🏠 Your Personal Locker (Dashboard)
Your private home page. It shows your all-time best lifts, your recent activity, and your overall progress at a glance.
🏋️ The Gym Floor (Log Workout)
This is where the work happens. You tell the app what exercise you did, how much weight you lifted, and how many times you lifted it.
🏆 The Scoreboard (Leaderboard)
A public wall showing the strongest people in the community. It adds a bit of friendly competition!
👤 The Mirror (Profile)
Where you change your profile picture and choose your username so your friends recognize you.
03 — What Happens When You Log a Workout?
1. You write it down
You type "100kg Bench Press" on your screen and hit save.
↓
2. The Waiter checks it
The app checks if you are actually logged in and allowed to save this data.
↓
3. The Vault secures it
The secure database permanently writes it down under your specific name.
↓
4. The Artist redraws
Your charts instantly update to show your new, higher progress line!
04 — How We Store Your Data
Our filing cabinet has three main drawers to keep things organized:
📋 The Member Directory
Holds your username, your unique ID, and a link to your profile picture.
📖 The Exercise Dictionary
A master list of every possible exercise (Squat, Deadlift, etc.) so everyone spells them the same way.
📓 The Master Logbook
The biggest drawer. It connects You to an Exercise, and saves the Weight, the Reps, and the Date.
